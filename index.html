Memory Game!

To play, please follow these instructions:

  1. There are 5 card color pairs. Your goal is to find all color pairs
  2. Start by clicking on any two cards.
  3. If they are the same color, you have found a match!
  4. If they don't match, they'll be hidden but their position won't change.
  5. Select cards 2 at a time until you've managed to find all the color pairings.
  6. Don't click more than 2 cards at the same time.
  7. Good luck!
